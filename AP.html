Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Do I have to join Discord to participate?
    Yes, but joining Discord is free, and it's a good idea anyway since it will give you access to a bunch of fun communities.
  2. How are all the APCSA units going to be covered?
    During each session we will cover 2-3 units depending on how heavy each one is.
  3. What will the sessions be like?
    Each session will be an hour, and 2-3 BAYCSC members will host it. We will cover some sample exam questions and cover common misconceptions.
  4. Who runs everything? The Bay Area Youth Computer Science Council runs the Discord, website, and sessions! We are a team of high schoolers with a passion for computer science.
  5. Who will the guest speakers be? We will announce guest speakers as we get them.
  6. By participating in this, can I put it on my resume? You can only put this resume if you become part of the organizing council by joining BAYCSC. We are always accepting new members!
  7. Will this get me a better AP test score? Maybe! It will definitely give you a studying structure.
  8. Can I ask a question at any time? Yes! BAYCSC members will try to respond to any questions you may have. You should also feel free to answer questions if you can help.
  9. Can I get help with homework? We can help you think through your homework and get resources, but we won't help you cheat or do it for you.
