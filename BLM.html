BAYCSC on Black Lives Matter

What do we think?

As we continue to educate ourselves about the topic, BAYCSC strongly stands in solidarity with the Black community and the BLM movement. As a team of high school students, BAYCSC is part of the future, meaning that the steps we choose to take now will impact what we see later on; therefore, change needs to begin now as it should have beforehand too. It is extremely important that we use our voices to take a stand for the black community, not just now, not just tomorrow, but within the actions we choose to make currently and in the future. BAYCSC strives to create a safe and welcoming environment for all and will continue to make a difference in our communities, especially of those that need it the most. Moving forward, we will dedicate time to educate our nearby communities as well as find ways to implement resources for the black community, a demographic that is underrepresented not just within BAYCSC, but also within all of STEM. There are various ways to take a step towards justice and to remove the unfair norms that exist today such as signing petitions, donating, or even starting conversations with peers. Listed below are various organizations and resources. Use your privilege as a way to fight the injustices. Everyone has a role to play, no matter how big or small.
